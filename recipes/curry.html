Description

A tumeric coconut-milk based dish made with chicken, potatoes, & carrots. Substitute chicken for cauliflower as a deliciously easy, vegeterian spin on this beloved traditional recipe!

Ingredients

  • 4 oz Red Curry Paste
  • 2 tbps Minced Garlic
  • 1 Red onion
  • 2 lbs Chicken Thighs or 1 whole Cauliflower
  • 1 can of Coconut Milk
  • 4 Potatoes
  • 2 Carrots
  • 1 bunch Parsley/Cilantro
  • 2 tbps avocado oil

Instructions

  1. Coat raw chicken thighs with 2 oz red curry paste.
  2. Set large frying pan to medium high heat
  3. Once hot, add avocado oil and spread evenly throughout pan.
  4. Add in minced garlic, diced red onions, and red curry paste, cook for 3-5 min until onions achieve desired crispiness.
  5. Apply chicken thighs to pan skin side first and sear for 6-8 min, then flip over.
  6. Add coconut milk, carrots, and potatoes, mix evenly, cover pan with lid, and allow to cook at low medium heat for 15-20 min.
  7. Once finished, garnish with parsley or cilantro
  8. Allow to cool, serve over a bed of warm, white rice and Enjoy!
