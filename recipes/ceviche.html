Description

A spicy, tangy ceviche made with shrimp, cucumber, serrano peppers, onions, and freshly squeezed citrus juices!

Ingredients

  • 4 serrano peppers
  • 3 jalapenos
  • 4 large cucumber
  • 2 lbs cooked shrimp
  • Juice of 6 limes
  • Juice of 3 Large Lemons
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 bunch Cilantro

Instructions

  1. Wash peppers, cut off tips, throw in jalapenos and serranos into blender or food processor
  2. Add in onion, cilantro, and juice of 2 limes and 1 lemon and blend
  3. Dice cucumber and cut white onion in half and cut into thin slices.
  4. Add shrimp, diced cucumbers, thinly sliced onion, and juice blend into bowl, mix thoroughly.
  5. Serve on crunchy tostada or tortilla chips with avocado on the side.
  6. Enjoy!
