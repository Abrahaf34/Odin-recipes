Description

An easy, nutritious and delicious breakfast made with greek yogurt, granola, bananas, and mixed berries ready to energize your mornings!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Greek Yogurt
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup of mixed berries
  • 1/2 cup of granola
  • 2 tbps Honey
  • 1 tbps Cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Add Greek yogurt to bowl or mason jar.
  2. Add Granola.
  3. Add mixed berries.
  4. Add Banana.
  5. Garnish with Honey and Cinnamon
  6. Enjoy!
